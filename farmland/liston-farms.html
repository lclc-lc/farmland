Liston Farms

Property Overview

Located west of Saint Anthony on the Egin Bench, Liston Farms comprises 84 acres of which 78 are irrigated with three pivot irrigation systems. This property also includes a 120,000 cwt potato cellar, making it an ideal operation for potato production.

Potato Storage Facility

The property includes a well-maintained 120,000 cwt potato cellar with modern temperature and humidity control systems. The storage facility features:

  • Automated climate control system with efficient forced-air ventilation
  • Drive-through design for easy loading/unloading
  • Built in 1994, upgraded insulation system installed in 2020

This storage facility provides significant added value and operational flexibility for the farm operation.

Premium Sandy Soils

The Liston Farms property is situated on the Egin Bench, known for its exceptional sandy loam soils that are particularly well-suited for potato production. These light soils warm quickly in the spring, allowing for earlier planting, and provide excellent drainage and tuber development conditions.

Residential Lots

For those interested, a home property of 17 acres, which includes a 7-acre mini pivot, a potential hobby farm or pasture is available for purchase to the northeast, suitable for farming or irrigated grazing. Additionally, a developable parcel of 2.75 acres is located to the southeast of Liston Farms.

Investment Opportunity

Liston Farms offers a unique investment opportunity with these key advantages:

  • Rare combination of irrigated acreage with on-site potato storage
  • Premium sandy soils ideal for potato production
  • Excellent location on the productive Egin Bench

This property would be ideal for a potato producer looking to expand operations with both growing area and storage capacity in one purchase.
